Join Us






FitNchill, as the name suggests, gives you an escape from your mundane life and opens the door to a world of adventure. We give you a chance to reconnect with nature, to explore new places and to bring out that sporty kid in you.

We do this by taking you to various trips either on foot or on a cycle where you can explore and enjoy the natural beauty.

Escapestories brings you an authentic travel experience which helps you to explore places like a traveller rather than a tourist who only visits well-known places.

And finally, we bring you various cycling events where you not only explore the places near you but also build better health.









What is Pedalsboost

All our cycling adventures are conducted under the name Pedalsboost.

It is more like a community of cycling enthusiasts where we motivate each other to pedal every day. Be it summer, winters or monsoon, we get out of the comfy bed to cycle. We cycle not only to keep ourselves fit but to enjoy the morning air, to celebrate our festivals, our national holidays, to enjoy the breakfast ride every now and then, to test our endurance on century rides or sometimes ride for 150km just to have parathas at Murthal :P. The list is long and the experiences can not be described in words. So, if you want to cycle and want to really enjoy it, then come and join our cycle tribe.
